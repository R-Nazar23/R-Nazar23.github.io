About Us

At Diamond Games Studio, we live and breathe the world of mobile gaming. Our journey began with a shared passion for creating unique and captivating gaming experiences. We're not just a studio; we're a close-knit team of dedicated individuals who believe in the magic of games. With each pixel crafted and each line of code written, we pour our creativity into crafting games that resonate with players. Our mission is simple: to bring joy, excitement, and a touch of wonder to the screens of gamers worldwide. Join us on this adventure as we continue to build dreams, one game at a time.

Why do we do our work? First of all, we want to give people the product to get joy, some funny spent time and positive emotions. From our side we may be willing to sell some advertisements to sustain our team. Despite of that, we hope that it will not affect users' gaming process and they will get all the emotions they expect to get.
